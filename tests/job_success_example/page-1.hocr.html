Workshop on the Tectonic Features on Mars, Washington, April 20-22

An LPI MEVTV Project workshop to address the questions “What are the geometry and kine- matics of tectonic features on Mars? What do surface structures tell us about the rheological or

mechanical properties of the near surface materi-

als, and the extent, if any, to which the rheology or mechanical properties are influenced by the presence of ground ice, permafrost, or volatile rich material? What can beinferred about the structure

of the lithosphere and crust through kinematic and mechanical models for the origin of certain structural features?” A field trip through the

s Yakima Fold Belt is planned for the workshop.

The meeting is sponsored by the Lunar and Plane- tary Institute. Conveners are Tom Watters (202——357-1457) and Matt Golombek (818-354- 3883). Contact either convener or Pam Jones. LPI (7130486—2l50) for for further information.
