In addition, Sphinxiocarpon wuhanium is obviously dis- tinguished from those seed—like reproductive organs of Carboniferous and Permian arborescent lycopsids, for example, Lepidocarpon lomaxii Scott (Balbach, 1965; Phillips, 1979), the sporangium of which is adaxially attached to the almost enclos- ing sporophyll by its longitudinal base (or the pad).
